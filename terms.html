Verification

4.1 During the first connection with us, we will require all users to go through the verification process in Sumsup. The verification process may vary depending on the amount of the transaction you wish to conduct with us.

For transactions under 1,000 euros, we request basic KYC verification. This includes providing a Proof of Identity from one of the following documents: ID card, Passport, Residence permit, or Travel document (not older than 3 months). If you provide a Travel document, we will also ask for a Proof of Address document. Acceptable documents include a bank letter, bank statement, mortgage payment, internet bill, etc.

For transactions between 1,000 euros and 15,000 euros, we require answers to two important questions:

  • Are you a Politically Exposed Person (PEP)?
  • Are you listed on any sanctions list?

We cannot provide services to individuals who are on PEP lists or any sanctions list. Additionally, Proof of Identity is required as part of this verification process.

For transactions over 15,000 euros, we request the following:

  • Purpose of the exchange
  • Source of funds (e.g., bank statements, payslips, tax returns, etc.)
  • Proof of Identity

This type of transaction will be reviewed manually.
